Hi! I’m Chen, a research scientist at the Technology Innovation Institute, working on large language models, natural language processing, and information retrieval. Before that, I completed my Ph.D. in Computer Science at the Weizmann Institute of Science , where I was advised by Prof. Robert Krauthgamer and Prof. Boaz Nadler. During my Ph.D., I interned at Booking.com and K Health, where I got to apply my research to personalized travel and healthcare chatbots.

These days, I’m especially interested in how we can get Gen-AI systems to provide more reliable, useful, and accessible information.

I’m always happy to talk about research, feel free to reach out!

Updates:

  • May 2025: Our paper on quantifying the distracting effect of irrelevant passages on LLMs has been accepted for oral presentation at ACL 2025. See you in Vienna!
